Starting Base

Building a Base — 8g
Order during Winter. Anchor ship must be uninterrupted for 1 full turn. If anchor ship is attacked, base construction restarts. If anchor ship is destroyed, 8g is lost.
Losing a Base
Bases can be destroyed by enemy combat ships. All ships using that base as their range anchor may become stranded.
Combat is initiated when enemy ships are on adjacent or same tiles after movement.
DISABLING ATTACK
Only vs fishing boats. Prevents fishing & movement for 1 turn. Does not destroy the ship.
LETHAL ATTACK
Destroys enemy ships when damage exceeds their HP. Combat units only.
Rows: Before combat, divide fleet into front/middle/back rows (roughly equal total HP). Hits are assigned to the frontmost occupied row.
FAC: Always has 50% chance to attack first, even against faster ships.
